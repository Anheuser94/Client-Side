BUDT748 INDUSTRY PRACTICUM

Semester Details: Fall 2025
Professor: Paul T. Shapiro
Teaching Assistants: Abhi Gupta, Samiha Meah, Zam Mozumder

BUDT748 is the capstone course for MS Information Systems (IS) majors, where students apply their knowledge to develop real-world solutions for business clients. Through a complete development process, students design and implement functional prototypes that solve practical business challenges, preparing them for careers in technology and consulting.
